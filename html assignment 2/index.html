Messi

He is a 35yr old argentinian footballer that currently plays in the french ligue 1 for Paris Saint Germain(PSG), wears the number 30 and plays on the left wing.
The argentine has won the UEFA Champions league four times with FC Barcelona and he was also able to lift the Copa America with the Argentine national squad in 2021, he is also an 8-time ballon d'or winner.
